Artist list

Quad Cortex

The Neural DSP Quad Cortex is a powerful floor modeler with a touchscreen, offering amp and effects modeling, IR loading, and "Neural Capture" for profiling real gear.
VOX AC30

The Vox AC30 is a legendary British tube amplifier known for its distinctive "chimey" high-end and warm overdrive. A 30-watt combo, it typically features two 12-inch Celestion speakers, offering Normal and Top Boost channels for a range of classic tones favored by iconic musicians.
Fuzz Face

The Fuzz Face: iconic, round fuzz pedal. Simple two knobs, delivers warm, thick, vintage distortion favored by guitar legends.
Jim Root's Signature Telecaster

Jim Root's signature white Telecaster: minimalist design, mahogany body, active EMGs for powerful, articulate high-gain tones.
Orange Rockerverb 100H MKIII

Orange Rockerverb 100H MKIII: versatile 100-watt tube head. Chimey cleans to heavy high-gain, with attenuator and power scaling.
Ibanez TOD10

The Ibanez TOD10 is Tim Henson's signature electric guitar, featuring Fishman Fluence pickups and a distinct "Tree of Death" inlay.
Ibanez TOD10n

Tim Henson's signature nylon-string: modified FR hollow body, Fishman electronics, "Tree of Death" inlay on a comfortable neck.
Schecter Synyster Gates Custom-S SBL

The Schecter Synyster Gates Custom-S in Satin Black Relic finish blends road-worn style with high-performance features, including hot pickups, a Floyd Rose tremolo, and a Sustainiac system.
Schecter Synyster Gates Standard Gloss

The Schecter Synyster Gates Standard Gloss delivers sleek looks and aggressive tone, featuring a glossy black finish, powerful pickups, and fast playability for modern metal and rock styles.
Axe-Fx III MKII Preamp/FX

The Axe-Fx III is a flagship amp modeler and effects processor, offering ultra-realistic tones, deep editing, and studio-grade processing for guitarists, bassists, and producers alike.
Ibanez ICHI10

The Ibanez ICHI10 is a sleek, modern electric guitar designed with Ichika Nito, featuring a unique tone, lightweight body, and exceptional playability for progressive and ambient styles.
Ibanez ICHI00

The Ibanez ICHI00 features a uniquely sculpted Talman-style body shape, offering a retro-modern look with ergonomic contours for comfortable playability and standout stage presence.
